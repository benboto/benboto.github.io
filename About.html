ABOUT ME

Hey there, welcome to my website! My name is Ben Carson.

I am 22 years old and I was born - and continue to live - in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada.

I have always have a passion for visual design and video games, but in the last few years I have grown tremendously, both in exposure to new ideas and mediums as well as my ability to make concepts come to life! I love creating, whether I'm coming up with ideas for game mechanics and game worlds or building spaceships and robots out of Lego.

My strengths are in idea generation, graphic design and game design. I have also had experience working with web design, 3D modeling and animation, industrial design, 3D printing, audio and video editing and more. I always look forward to improving my skills, learning new things and looking for inspiration in films, games and literature.

EDUCATION

I am a recent graduate of the Interactive Multimedia and Design program at Carleton University in collaboration with Algonquin College.

During my time in the IMD program, I worked on a variety of different projects, both on my own and as part of a larger group. These projects ranged from simple 2D animation, user interface design, web mockups, practical websites, poster and logo design and branding to 3D modeling, rendering and animation, game design, game development, product testing and evaluation and audio and video editing.

I also spent a significant portion of my final year working on a complex team project; creating a multiplayer video game titled Pulse Fighters. Not only was I responsible for the development of a unique interactivity system, promotional content and some asset creation, but I also took on the responsibility of being the team leader. This was a huge undertaking for everyone involved and taught me a lot about project development as well as time and group management. You can read more about this and other projects in the portfolio section of this site.
