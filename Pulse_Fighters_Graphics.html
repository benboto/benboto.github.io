PULSE FIGHTERS

Project Type: Academic - Team Based

Status: Complete

Time Period: Winter Term 2015 (January - April)

Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Pulse Fighters is an action arcade brawler game, in the style of games like Super Smash Brothers. The game was developed by myself and 5 other of my university peers under the team name 'Windchill Studios'. You can find out more about the game by visting the game design section of my portfolio covering Pulse Fighters (Coming Soon), or by visiting the promotional website (Jesse Leitch, 2015).

This section of my portfolio will showcase the work that I did on promotional content for the game, as well as user interface assets that I created for PC and mobile project components.

*The Pulse Fighters Logo, seen on the right hand side of the banner, was designed by Brecon White and Alex McAvoy

Further elaborations, image captions and process descriptions are on the way, check here for more info! Thank you for your patience.
